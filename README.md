# Food Connect App

Food Connect is a web application that connects strangers who want to have food together and share their food experiences. The app allows users to discover nearby food partners, chat, plan meals, and post shared food experiences.

## Features

*   **Discover Food Partners:** Find people nearby who are interested in sharing a meal.
*   **Chat:** Connect with other foodies through a real-time chat.
*   **Plan Meals:** Organize and schedule meals with your new friends.
*   **Share Experiences:** Post photos and stories of your shared meals.

## Tech Stack

*   HTML
*   CSS
*   JavaScript
